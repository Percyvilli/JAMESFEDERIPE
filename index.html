How I Work With Clients

My clear, transparent 4-step process makes collaboration smooth, predictable, and stress-free.

1

Initial Discussion & Understanding Your Needs

We begin with a quick, friendly conversation (email, Telegram, Messenger, or a short Zoom call — your choice). I ask questions and really listen so I fully understand:

  • Which tasks are taking up most of your time
  • What specific outcomes you want to achieve
  • Your preferred way of communicating and updating
  • Any tools, passwords, or access you already use
  • Your timeline, budget range, and priorities

This first step is always free and no-obligation — usually 15–40 minutes. The goal is complete clarity before any commitment.

2

Planning & Written Agreement

After our talk, I prepare a short, easy-to-read proposal that includes:

  • Exact tasks/services I will handle
  • Estimated weekly/monthly hours or fixed package
  • How and how often I'll send updates (daily messages, weekly summary, etc.)
  • My rate and simple payment terms
  • Start date and any tools/access needed

You review it, ask for changes if needed, and we both agree before I start any paid work. Everything is written clearly — no surprises.

3

Execution & Consistent Updates

I begin working using your preferred tools and style. During active work you can expect:

  • Regular progress updates (daily quick messages or weekly detailed reports)
  • Fast replies to any questions or new requests
  • Proactive ideas if I notice better/faster ways to do something
  • Full transparency — you always know what's done and what's next

I only proceed with major decisions after your approval. You stay in control at all times.

4

Review, Feedback & Continuous Improvement

At the end of each week, project phase, or task batch:

  • I deliver all finished work (files, organized folders, inbox screenshots, reports, etc.)
  • We do a short review (message or quick call)
  • You share what you liked and what could be improved
  • I make any adjustments immediately
  • We decide next steps — continue, change scope, or pause

My main goal is your complete satisfaction. If anything isn't right, I fix it quickly at no extra charge.
